I'm a final-year Computer Science student at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, interested in AI research. My work so far centres on large language models — evaluating their creativity on open-ended tasks, the tension between hallucination and creativity, and speech understanding across Southeast Asia.

More broadly, I'm drawn to deep learning, natural language processing, and multimodal AI, and I'm hoping to pursue research after graduation.
