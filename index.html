I'm a fourth-year Computer Science student at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, on the CN Yang Scholars Programme, interested in AI research. My work so far centres on large language models — evaluating their creativity on open-ended tasks, the tension between hallucination and creativity, and speech understanding across Southeast Asia.

I'm currently a research intern at MBZUAI, working with Prof. Preslav Nakov on LLM agents for assessing source trustworthiness. More broadly, I'm drawn to deep learning, natural language processing, and multimodal AI, and I'm hoping to pursue research after graduation.
