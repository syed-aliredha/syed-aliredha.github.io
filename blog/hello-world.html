Hello, world

4 Jul 2026 meta

Welcome to the blog. The plan for this space is to write about things I'm reading and working on in AI research — LLM evaluation, creativity and hallucination, speech models for Southeast Asia, and whatever else catches my attention along the way.

What to expect

Roughly three kinds of posts:

  • Paper notes — short summaries of papers I found worth the read.
  • Research threads — longer-form thoughts on open questions.
  • Build logs — occasional notes on tools and projects, like this site.

A small note on the design

This site is hand-written HTML, CSS, and a little JavaScript — no framework, no build step. Posts are plain HTML pages, so anything works in them: links like this one, images, and quotes:

The real problem is not whether machines think but whether men do.
— B. F. Skinner
